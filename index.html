Julia MacDonald

I work on the human side of AI: training data and operations. Currently at Haize Labs. Previously VP of AI Operations at SuperAnnotate. I've helped 150+ startups and Fortune 500s build scalable AI workflows.

I focus on frontier AI developments — especially post-training. Recipes, RL environments, and the human data that feeds them: who produces it, how, and at what price. And increasingly, AI skills: what the rest of us need to learn as AI becomes our teacher, not just our tool.

The other half of my week is founders. I've spent years helping early-stage teams - computer vision, web3, biotech - with AI strategy, operations, and growth, and I guest lecture at Cal.

AI Operations · Human Data · Frontier Models

Julia MacDonald

I work on the human side of frontier AI: the training data, evaluation loops, and operations that decide whether models actually get better. Currently at Haize Labs; previously VP of AI Operations at SuperAnnotate, where training data quality was the product. Along the way I've helped 150+ startups and Fortune 500s build AI workflows that survive contact with production.

My current research focus is the state of human data in AI — what multimodal models will need next (prosody, intent, emotion), how RL environments get built and priced, and where agent memory should live. I write about this below and speak about it regularly. MBA from UC Berkeley's Haas School of Business; guest lecturer at Cal. I speak five languages — English, German, Russian, Armenian, and French — which is useful when your field is teaching machines to understand humans.

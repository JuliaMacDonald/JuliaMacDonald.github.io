Research dinners at Haize Labs

March 2026 Events & Research Haize Labs · FiDi

At Haize Labs events, the food is almost as good as the conversations about self-distillation fine-tuning.

We hosted another research evening at the Haize Labs office in FiDi, and the topic that took over the room was self-distillation fine-tuning — the idea that a model can teach itself by learning from its own outputs, and what that means for continual learning without catastrophic forgetting.

The paper behind the discussion: Self-Distillation Enables Continual Learning. Worth your time if you care about models that keep learning after deployment instead of being frozen at training time.

Thank you Albert Chun & AI Circle for the amazing partnership 🤝 — and stay tuned: the next one is in a month, same place.

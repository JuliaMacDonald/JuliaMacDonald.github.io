Generative AI for e-commerce — on stage in Miami

May 2025 Speaking Miami AI Hub

I joined the AI in E-Commerce Forum in Wynwood as a panelist on "Generative AI Solutions for E-Commerce" — one of my favorite topics, and one of the places where AI is quietly reshaping an entire industry.

The forum, powered by Wasserman Digital and Miami AI Hub, brought retailers, founders, and operators together at Northeastern University Miami. My angle came from years of running AI operations: what separates the teams whose generative AI pilots make it to production from the ones whose demos never leave the boardroom.

Having helped 150+ startups and Fortune 500s build scalable AI workflows — a lot of them in e-commerce — my main message was simple: the model is the easy part. The hard part is the operational loop around it — the data you feed it, the guardrails you wrap it in, and the people who own the output.
